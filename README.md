# 🏠 Baytak

> A transparent and commission-free apartment rental platform built to connect renters directly with apartment owners

## ⭐ Features
- ✅ **Direct communication** with the apartment owner — no middleman involved  
- 🚫 **No broker** or hidden fees — full transparency  
- 💸 **Zero commissions** — save your money  
- ⚡ **Fast & easy-to-use UI** — suitable for all age groups  
- 🔍 **Advanced search filters** — quickly find your perfect apartment  
- 📱 **Fully responsive** — works seamlessly on all devices  
- 🔐 **Secure by design** — your data is safe, both for sellers and customers  

---

## 📌 Target Problem

> In many places, especially in Egypt and the Arab world, apartment seekers suffer from high broker commissions and lack of transparency.  
**Baytak** solves this by providing a direct bridge between apartment owners and renters — simple, fair, and commission-free.
